# Meridian Capital Partners — Treasury Data Review & Reporting

## Case Exercise Brief

### Context

You have recently joined the Treasury team at Meridian Capital Partners, a private equity firm managing ~$45B across 8 active funds. Your first assignment is to review the April 2026 treasury data, build a consolidated cash position report, and document any issues you find.

You have been given four files:

| File | Description |
|---|---|
| `fund_cash_positions.xlsx` | Cash balances across all funds, banks, currencies, and account types |
| `credit_facility_summary.xlsx` | Subscription credit facility details and covenant status |
| `wire_transfer_log.csv` | Wire transfer activity for April 2026 |
| `README_treasury_context.md` | Treasury team policies, FX rates, approval thresholds, and reporting context |

**Read the README carefully before starting.** It contains policies and standards that are essential to this exercise.

---

### Deliverables

You must submit the following:

#### 1. Consolidated Cash Position Report

Build a clean, presentation-ready summary of Meridian's total cash position as of April 30, 2026. The report should include:

- Total cash by fund
- Breakdown by currency (with USD equivalents)
- Breakdown by account type (Operating, Reserve, Distribution)
- Credit facility available capacity by fund
- Total liquidity view (cash + available credit)

Format: Excel or Google Sheets. The report should be clear enough for the CFO to review without additional explanation.

#### 2. Issues & Findings Memo

Write a 1–2 page memo documenting all issues, discrepancies, or risks you identified in the data. For each issue:

- **Describe** what you found
- **Explain** why it matters
- **Assign a severity level:** Blocking / High / Medium / Low
- **Recommend** a specific action

Severity definitions:
- **Blocking:** Would corrupt data or cause a fundamental failure. Must be resolved before any downstream work proceeds.
- **High:** Real risk to data integrity or compliance. Must have an explicit mitigation before proceeding.
- **Medium:** Should be handled, but a controlled cleanup can follow initial delivery.
- **Low:** Worth noting. No immediate action required.

Format: PDF or Word document.

#### 3. Video Walkthrough (5 minutes max)

Record a short video (Loom or similar) walking through:
- Your key findings
- How you prioritized them
- What you would do next if this were your first week on the job

This is not a presentation — it's a conversation. Speak naturally and explain your thinking.

#### 4. AI Use Disclosure

Include a brief note (can be at the end of your memo or as a separate document) describing:
- Which deliverables and decisions involved AI assistance
- How you used AI tools (if at all)
- What parts of the work are entirely your own

**AI use is permitted.** The disclosure is about transparency, not restriction. We evaluate the quality of judgment and the accuracy of the work, regardless of tools used.

---

### Time Limit

**This exercise is designed to be completed in 4–6 hours.** Please do not spend more than 6 hours. If you find yourself running out of time, prioritize:

1. The Issues & Findings Memo (most important)
2. The Consolidated Report
3. The Video Walkthrough

We are evaluating your judgment and analytical rigor, not your ability to produce volume under pressure.
